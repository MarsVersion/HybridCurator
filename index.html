I'm a curator, writer, and now creative technologist.

I used to run the art space Diskurs Berlin and write about art for magazines. My curatorial work has taken me across Europe and Asia, collaborating with institutions like Kunstmuseum Bochum, Asia Triennial Manchester, and Mediations Biennale.

In 2024, I began combining my curatorial practice with coding. That led to Busy Mars — an AI-powered platform designed to help art professionals and institutions connect and discover overlooked voices in art and tech.
