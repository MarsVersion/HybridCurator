Jung Me Chai – hybrid curator

Contemporary art curator turned technologist — building tools, platforms, and strategies at the intersection of culture and code.

Built with care

Every system I build is designed with care for the people who will use it. Whether it's an artist looking for visibility, a curator seeking new voices, or an audience member discovering work that moves them.

Visibility

Technology should make hidden things visible. I focus on creating tools that surface underrepresented artists, ideas, and perspectives that might otherwise remain unseen.

Think in systems

I think in systems, not just individual solutions. How do the tools we build connect to broader cultural ecosystems? How do they support sustainable, equitable practices?
