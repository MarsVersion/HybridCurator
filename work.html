Work With Me

A bridge between curating and code.

I work at the intersection of curating, research, and technology—supporting institutions, collectives, and individuals navigating today's cultural and technological shifts. My focus is on developing thoughtful strategies, tools, and platforms grounded in care, complexity, and access.

Art & AI Tools

  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for cultural archives

Tech Consulting for Art Professionals

  • Platform audits and digital strategy for institutions
  • Workflow and content structure consulting
